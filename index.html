ABOUT HOTEL


Century Park Hotel Jakarta was established in 1991 and stands as the top of the line 4 star luxury business hotels in Senayan area Jakarta. This hotel in Senayan is near the Jakarta Convention Center / JCC, consisting of 476 spacious guest rooms and suites which reflect the spacious grandeur of its surroundings, offering a lush green view, wealth of activities and

a high level of service. Conveniently located in the center of Jakarta, our hotel in Senayan is the perfect venue for all your occasions. Our banquet and event team will assist you in organizing a memorable experience, whilst our superior culinary team will offer flexibility in creating the perfect menu.


The hotel is only a short stroll from Gelora Bung Karno Stadium, Senayan Driving Range, Senayan National Golf Club, and the
prestigious shopping malls. It is near Senayan City Jakarta, Plaza Senayan, and fx Sudirman.


Check in time at 2.00 pm and Check out time at 12.00 pm.
Room cancellation should be received 24-hour prior to arrival date. Cancellation policy maybe differs for each room and rates. Please
check for details at the time of booking.


Location:
• 45 minutes to the international airport.
